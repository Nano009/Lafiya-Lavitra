About Us

Our Goal

Our goal is to create an informative website dedicated to the well-being of teenagers. We believe that maintaining a healthy diet is not just essential for physical health but also significantly influences academic success and mental well-being. We wish to promote and encourage healthy eating habits and offer practical tips so that teenagers may still excel in their studies without compromising their well-being.

Our History

The story started when a team of three individuals - Nano, Pwar Pwar, and La Wun - saw that young students in today's digital age tend to spend excessive time confined to their rooms, often overlooking their physical health and overall well-being.

This sparked an idea within their minds and they wanted to educate young students about the significance of maintaining good health and preventing diseases, which lead to this website being designed and developed.

Today, they hard work had paid off and thousands of teenagers have become beneficiaries of their goal.
